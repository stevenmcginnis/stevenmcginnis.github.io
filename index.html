Hi, thanks for visiting my website. I’m Steven McGinnis, an aspiring Data Scientist currently studying for a Higher Diploma in Science in Data Analytics from Dublin Business School.

My journey into Data Science began with a BA in Economics from UCC, where in a module on Econometrics I discovered my love for deriving insights from data. In the modern world, the generation and application of information is a crucial economic activity. Data Science facilitates this with its potential to extract information from large volumes of data.

What I find most alluring about working in Data Science is the opportunity to work on a constant stream of new and challenging puzzles. I have been asking "why" and "how" since I could speak the words.

If you would like to see some of my work check out myportfolio, orcontact me to hear about some of the projects I'm currently working on.
